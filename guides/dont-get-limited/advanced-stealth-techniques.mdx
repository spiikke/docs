## Multi-Account Coordination

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Family/Household Strategy">

If multiple eligible adults live in your household, coordinate your betting to maximize household profits.

**Example:** Spouse focuses on quick-limit books while you focus on slow-limit books.

</Tab>

<Tab title="Geographic Diversification">

If you travel frequently, take advantage of different state offerings.

**Strategy:** Complete sign-ups in multiple states where you spend time legally.

</Tab>

## Advanced Camouflage Techniques

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Intentional Small Losses" icon="trending-down">
Occasionally place small bets you expect to lose to show "normal" losing patterns.

**Example:** Bet $20 on a heavy underdog without hedging once per month.
</Card>

<Card title="Parlay Participation" icon="layer-group">
Occasionally bet small parlays (which have high house edge) to appear recreational.

**Caution:** Keep these very small ($5-$10) as they're -EV bets.
</Card>

<Card title="Social Betting Features" icon="users">
Engage with social features like following other bettors or sharing bets.

**Benefit:** Shows you're engaged with the platform beyond just extracting value.
</Card>

<Card title="Live Betting Participation" icon="play">
Occasionally place small live bets during games you're watching.

**Appears natural:** Recreational bettors often bet impulsively during games.
</Card>
</CardGroup>

## Technology Considerations

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Device Management" icon="laptop">
- Use consistent devices when possible
- Don't switch between many different IPs
- Keep location services enabled
- Use the official mobile apps
</Card>

<Card title="Browser/App Behavior" icon="globe">
- Clear cookies occasionally but not obsessively
- Don't use VPNs unless traveling
- Allow normal tracking/analytics
- Don't use automation tools
</Card>
</CardGroup>
