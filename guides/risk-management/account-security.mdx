## Protecting Your Accounts

Account security goes beyond passwords. Proper security practices protect your bankroll, personal information, and ensure long-term access to profitable opportunities.

## Account Setup Best Practices

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Do This">

- Use your real name and information
- Provide accurate address and phone
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Verify accounts immediately
- Keep documents ready for verification

</Tab>

<Tab title="Never Do This">

- Use fake names or information
- Share accounts with others
- Use VPNs to bypass location restrictions
- Create multiple accounts per person
- Use someone else's payment methods
- Ignore verification requests

</Tab>
</Tabs>

## Payment Method Security

**Recommended Payment Methods:**

- **Primary Options:** Debit cards (fastest withdrawals), Online banking (ACH transfers), PayPal (where accepted)
- **Avoid:** Credit cards (cash advance fees), Prepaid cards (verification issues), Cryptocurrency (regulatory concerns)

<Tip>
**Payment Security Tips:**

- Use the same payment method for deposits and withdrawals
- Keep payment methods in your name only
- Monitor all transactions regularly
- Report any unauthorized activity immediately
- Keep records of all deposits and withdrawals
</Tip>

## Privacy and Data Protection

**Personal Information Management:**

- Use a dedicated email for betting accounts
- Keep personal documents secure and organized
- Be cautious about sharing betting activities publicly
- Understand each sportsbook's privacy policy
- Regularly review account settings and permissions

**Tax and Legal Considerations:**

- Keep detailed records of all winnings and losses
- Understand tax obligations in your jurisdiction
- Save all 1099 forms and tax documents
- Consider consulting a tax professional
- Stay informed about changing regulations

<Warning>
**Warning Signs to Watch For:**

- Unexpected account restrictions or closures
- Unusual login attempts or security alerts
- Discrepancies in account balances
- Changes in available promotions
- Requests for additional documentation
- Delayed withdrawals or payment issues
</Warning>
