## Protecting Your Investment

Proper bankroll management is essential for long-term success in matched betting. Even "risk-free" strategies carry some risks that must be managed carefully.

## Bankroll Sizing Guidelines

<Card title="Conservative" icon="shield">

- Build slowly over time
- Lower risk tolerance

## Risk Management Rules

<CardGroup cols={3}>
<Card title="The 5% Rule" icon="percent">
Never risk more than 5% of your total bankroll on any single promotion or bet. This protects against catastrophic losses from human error or unexpected outcomes.
</Card>

<Card title="The 2x Coverage Rule" icon="layer-group">
Always maintain at least 2x the bet amount across all your sportsbook accounts to ensure you can properly hedge any position.
</Card>

<Card title="The Emergency Fund" icon="piggy-bank">
Keep 10-20% of your bankroll as an emergency fund for unexpected situations, account issues, or exceptional opportunities.
</Card>
</CardGroup>

## Distribution Strategy

**Recommended Bankroll Allocation:**

- **Primary Books (3-4 major sportsbooks):** 60-70%
- **Secondary Books (2-3 additional books):** 20-25%
- **Emergency Reserve:** 10-15%

<Warning>
**Avoid These Mistakes:**

- Putting all funds in one sportsbook
- Not maintaining adequate hedge book balances
- Chasing losses with larger bets
- Withdrawing too frequently (triggers scrutiny)
</Warning>
