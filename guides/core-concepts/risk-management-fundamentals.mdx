While matched betting is designed to be risk-free, proper risk management ensures you protect your profits and avoid common pitfalls.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Common Risks to Avoid" icon="alert-triangle">
- Human error in calculations or bet placement
- Account limitations or closures
- Voided bets or rule changes
- Insufficient bankroll management
</Card>

<Card title="Best Practices" icon="check-circle">
- Always double-check calculations before placing bets
- Start with smaller amounts until comfortable
- Keep detailed records of all bets and profits
- Diversify across multiple sportsbooks
</Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
The primary risk in matched betting is human error. Always double-check your bets before confirming.
</Tip>
